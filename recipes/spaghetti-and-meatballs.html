Spaghetti and Meatballs

Spaghetti and Meatballs is the cornerstone dish of Italy. Nothing fills the stomach quite like it! Pair the dish with a fine wine and share a nice meal with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 cup of olive oil
  • 1 lb Groud Pork
  • 1 lb Groud Beef
  • 1 cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 3 tbsp Salt
  • 1 sprig of thyme
  • 1 sprig of rosemary
  • 1 sprig of basil
  • 2 32oz cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Step By Step

  1. Chop up all of the thyme, rosemary, basil, and garlic. Place them all in a large mixing bowl
  2. Mix the ground pork and the ground beef together, add the meat into the large mixing bowl
  3. Add the salt and parmesan cheese into the large mixing bowl
  4. Mix everything together and roll into small balls (about the size of a ping pong ball)
  5. Begin to boil a large pot of water, around 3-4 quarts is fine
  6. Heat up the olive oil until you start to see little wisps of smoke rising from the pan
  7. Heat up the sauce in a pot on medium heat until it starts to bubble up a little bit
  8. Place the balls into the pan to start to brown them up. Rotate the balls until all of the sides are fully browned
  9. Once fully browned, place meatballs into the hot spaghetti sauce and let the pot continue to simmer
  10. When the pot begins to boil, add in the spaghetti noodles and follow the cooking instructions found on the package, add salt to taste
  11. Drain the pasta when finished, and enjoy!
