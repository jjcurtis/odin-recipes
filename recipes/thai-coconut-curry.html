Thai Coconut Curry

This dish is very versatile and is a favorite among our family. Add as much Thai chili powder as you can handle!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 1 15 oz can of coconut milk (can be full or reduced fat)
  • 1 15 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 lb diced chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp Thai chili powder
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped green onion
  • 2 chopped green bell peppers
  • 1 sprig of cilantro
  • 1 sprig of Thai basil
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Step By Step

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions, add the bay leaves to flavor the rice as it cooks. Also add salt to taste
  2. Heat up the coconut oil on medium heat in a large skillet. When hot, add the chicken breast and brown the meat a little. Salt to taste!
  3. Once browned, add the coconut milk, tomatoes, salt, carrots, bell peppers, and onions to the skillet and cook for around 12 minutes
  4. Add the red chili flakes, Thai basil, and the chili powder to the skillet
  5. Once the sauce is nice and thick and the chicken is cooked through, add the green onions, cilantro, and garlic to the skillet to cook for a minute or two
  6. Serve it up with some of the rice you made, and enjoy!
